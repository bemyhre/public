About Codefusion
Codefusion was my capstone project at Dev Bootcamp. This application allows a teacher to broadcast his code (either a file or a folder) using whatever text-editor he wants. Then, students can watch the instructor and take notes on a file by file basis. When desired, the student can download the entire project locally into a zip file.
Technologies used: Ruby (ruby gem), Firebase, Ember, Javascript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3
Fifty Shades of Bacon
Yup... I made this. One day, the idea popped into my head. Even though I had never even considered writing a book before, by the end of that day, I decided I was going to write a cookbook and I did. In less than 2 months, the final project was for sale on Amazon and has went on to be a best seller in the Meats category in Amazon since first brough to market nearly 2 years ago. It has brought interesting opportuniities, including an interview with Seattle Kitchen, an article in Allure magazine and being known as the bacon guy by way too many people.
